About Us

Market Deeping based beauty clinic, Deep-In Aesthetics was formed by father of two, Alistair Graham.

Alistair trained at Harley Street as an aesthetic practioner.

As an independent clinic we select from the best techniques and products to provide high quality treatment with superior results

Our approach is to provide a full free, no obligation consultation with Alistair, within this medical history is looked at and all options are discussed in complete confidence.

There is a cooling-off period where the client can go away and think about the options discussed and this will allow the customer to make a measured and informed decision.
